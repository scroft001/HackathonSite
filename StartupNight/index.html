Startup Night

A night of million dollar ideas

Hackathon Request

How it works

Each participant comes to the event with a beautiful website that sells their idea. The website is there to be the visuals in their presentation. The kids would stay with their computer during the open floor pitching section. During this time the parents, with fake money, would go around the room and "invest" in the opportunities they feel are the most beneficial or show the highest return. The top 3 would then get a chance to pitch their idea to the entire crowd after which everyone votes on the top idea. The winner receives a prize and some Hackingtons showcase love.

Why Websites?

By putting the focus on building websites as the visual element instead of a poster board we show the kids that the web is really where business ideas live and breath. Even if you have a so-so idea if your website is amazing it can sell the product for you. It also illustrates the importance of UI design, if the buyer can't find the product they can't purchase it. This event also gives the more visually focused students a chance to showcase their expertise. You don't have to be a coding genius to build an amazing website.
